About Us

Siena College of Quezon City is a well-established educational institution in the Philippines, known for its strong Catholic foundation and commitment to holistic education. Founded in 1959 by the Dominican Sisters of St. Catherine of Siena, the college offers a wide array of programs that cater to both basic and higher education. It is located in San Francisco del Monte, Quezon City, and serves as a beacon of quality learning and faith-based values in the region.

The college provides diverse academic programs, including degrees in Tourism, Business Administration, Hotel and Restaurant Management, Mass Communications, and Religious Education. Additionally, it offers primary, secondary, and specialized education programs like Special Education (SPED), alongside its regular undergraduate courses.

Siena College emphasizes its commitment to fostering academic excellence, spiritual growth, and social responsibility among its students. It actively participates in various educational networks, including affiliations with the Catholic Educational Association of the Philippines and the Philippine Accrediting Association of Schools, Colleges, and Universities (PAASCU)

Our Courses

Tourism

Unlock a dynamic career in tourism with Siena College Quezon City's Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management. Gain practical skills in travel, destination planning, and hospitality, preparing you for the global tourism industry.

Business Administration

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration program prepares students for leadership roles in various fields, including Financial Management, Marketing, Human Resources, and Operations. Gain the skills needed for a successful business career.

Hotel and Restaurant Management

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management offers comprehensive training in Hotel and Restaurant Management. Students gain practical experience and theoretical knowledge to excel in the dynamic hospitality industry, focusing on operations, customer service, and event management.

Mass Communications

Bachelor of Arts in Communication equips students with the skills necessary for careers in media, public relations, advertising, and journalism. The program focuses on enhancing communication strategies, critical thinking, and content creation, preparing graduates for success in the fast-paced communication industry.

Religious Education

Master of Arts in Religious Education is designed for those passionate about teaching and spreading religious knowledge. The program offers in-depth studies in theology, pastoral ministry, and educational practices, preparing graduates to lead and educate within religious communities.p>
