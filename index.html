Mission-Vision Statement

Mission

Mission Inspired by the virtues and values of Ven. Mother Francisca del Espiritu Santo de Fuentes,
the community of Siena College, Inc., Quezon City commits itself to the integral formation of persons
promoting a quality Catholic Dominican Filipino Sienan education.

Vision

We envision to be an innovative, relevant, and resilient provider of quality Catholic,
Dominican, Filipino, Sienan education in the spirit of communion, participation, and mission.
